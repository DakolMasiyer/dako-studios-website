## Value

One sentence: Build something real, not just a certificate — structured, hands-on creative training with a portfolio piece at the end, taught with the same studio DNA as the rest of Dako.

## Voice

Do:
- Plain text, conversational, short sentences.
- UK English spelling and idiom throughout.
- Encouraging and concrete — speak to what the learner will be able to make, not abstract promises.
- Read like one founder writing to another, not a template.

Don't:
- No unnecessary hyphenation — let sentences run naturally rather than chaining hyphenated phrases.
- No links, logos, or attachments in the first email (deliverability).
- No fake urgency ("only 2 spots left", "offer ends tonight").
- No guarantees about career or income outcomes.
- No overly formal or corporate register.
