We managed the entire lifecycle of The Shameless Podcast's transformation. This began with a complete visual rebranding, establishing a refined logo and comprehensive brand guidelines to ensure a consistent, professional identity across all touchpoints.

### Brand Guidelines & Identity
We developed a cohesive brand kit outlining the core visual language, typography, and colour palette that define the show's renewed presence.

### Production & Motion Assets
Beyond static visuals, our team handled end-to-end technical production — scripting, guest scheduling, final editing, and dynamic motion assets including polished episode intros that elevated the show's production value.

### Digital Marketing & Social Growth
To amplify reach, we executed a targeted digital marketing strategy, repurposing core episodes into social content, newsletters, and blogs. The result was measurable growth in audience retention and Spotify listenership.
